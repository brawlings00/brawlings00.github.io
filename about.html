Brianna Rawlings

Who Is She?

Brianna is originally from Las Vegas, Nevada, but recently moved to Noblesville, Indiana with her two parents and small dog. Brianna enjoys spending time outside fishing, hiking, and camping. She also enjoys country music, hanging out with friends, and eating ice cream.

Brianna is a Computer Science-Digital Media student with minors in data science and sports management at Taylor University, where she also serves as an athlete and captain for the Women's Soccer Team. She is passionate about web design/development, sports management, digital media, data analytics, and full-stack development.

Currently, she works as a Jr. Course Designer at Taylor University Online, contributing to the design of the learning management system, Brightspace. In this role, she leverages her skills of time management, leadership, and communication to enhance the online learning experience.

In her second year at Taylor University, Brianna is honing valuable skills including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, that will pave the way for success in her future endeavors. She is actively seeking internship opportunities for the summer of 2024, particularly in areas intersecting sports, technology, analytics, and design.
